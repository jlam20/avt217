Programmer and Computer

Instructions

  1. Open Adobe XD (or whichever program you are designing in).
  2. Create an artboard that is 500px in width and 500px in height.
  3. Make the fill of the art board #FFFFFF.
  4. Click on the polygon tool.
  5. Click and drag on the artboard to create a triangle (it should have a corner count of 3).
  6. Adjust the width and height of the triangle to be 150px.
  7. Change the border size of the triangle to 2 and the border color to #707070.
  8. Move the triangle to the center of the artboard (the X and Y position should be 175).
  9. Click on the line tool.
  10. Click and drag on the artboard to create a vertical line.
  11. Adjust the height of the line to be 150px.
  12. Change the border size of the line to 2 and the border color to #707070.
  13. Move the line to the center of the triangle (the X position should be 250.5 and the Y position should be 175.5). The line should run down the middle of the triangle vertically and should not go outside of the triangle's edges.
  14. Save and export this artboard.
  15. Email the artboard png file to jlam20@masonlive.gmu.edu.
